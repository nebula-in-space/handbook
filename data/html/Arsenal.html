    Weapons

    What follows below is a table of some common or robust items that may help you win, separated by category. Uncommon weapons may not be listed and the ones that are could be outdated, so don't rely entirely on the table. If in doubt, examining the weapon and clicking the "bluespace tag" will tell you how many hits it takes to bring someone down.

    Дальнобойное Оружие

    Although often hard to get, these can put you at a huge advantage. Not just from being outside melee range, but also by shooting through windows and destroying walls. Guns rely on a stock of ammo, and those are hard to get, while energy guns use recharging units or just recharge by themselves.

    Энергетическое

    Energy weapons are among the most common kind of weapon you'll find on a Nanotrasen space station. Reliable but has issues with ammo.

    Shotgun Shells

    • Improvised Shotgun Shell: Shoots a spread of 7 metal shards, doing a total of 42 damage at point blank. Has damage falloff from range.
    • Buckshot: Shoots 6 pellets dealing 7.5 brute damage each, for a maximum of 45. Has damage falloff from range.
    • Slug: Shoots a single 50 damage shot.
    • Rubber Shot: Shoots 6 pellets dealing 3 brute and 11 stamina damage each. Known to bounce off walls and other things, useful in maintenance
    • Beanbag: Shoots one single beanbag, dealing 10 brute and 55 stamina damage. The bartender uses these by default.
    • Breaching Slug: Shoots a single shot that does very little damage to living things (5 brute), but huge damage to structures like windows. Good for breaking into places. Available to the Space IRS pirate crew.
    • Stun Shell: Shoots an electrode that stuns on hit. (Not easily available)
    • Pulverizer Slug: Shoots a single shot that inflicts huge blunt wounds. Only spawnable by admins.
    • Executioner Slug: Shoots a single shot that inflicts huge slash wounds. Only spawnable by admins.
    • Custom Incapacitating Shot: Shoots 12 pellets dealing only 1 brute, but 6 stamina per pellet. Used for incapacitating someone. Can only be loaded in hook-modified sawn-off shotguns and are available to hook bounty hunters.
    • Incendiary Slug: Shoots a single projectile that leaves a fire trail and ignites the target, on top of 20 brute damage.
    • Precision Incendiary Slug: Same as the regular incendiary slug, but does not leave a fire trail.
    • Shotgun Dart: A single dart that can contain up to 15 units of chemicals that'll be injected on hit.
    • Technological Shells: Unloaded technological shells can be made in a Protolathe. See here for the recipes.
      • Meteorshot Shell: A single powerful projectile, deals 30 brute damage, stuns for 1.5 seconds and causes knockdown for 8 seconds. Pushes back machinery and some structures on hit.
      • Pulse Slug: A weaker version of one of the deathsquad's pulse rifle shots, dealing 30 burn damage and damaging or destroying walls and machinery.
      • Dragonsbreath Shell: Shoots a spread of 4 burning pellets, dealing 5 damage each.
      • FRAG-12 Slug: An explosive shell that paralyzes targets for 1 seconds, causes a small explosion where it hits, while also dealing 15 extra brute damage.
      • Ion Scatter Shell: Shoots a spread of 4 ion bolts, each of which ions a single target instead of the 3x3 area hit by normal ion rifles.
      • Laser Scatter Shell: Shoots a spread of 6 laser beams with up to 90 total damage.
